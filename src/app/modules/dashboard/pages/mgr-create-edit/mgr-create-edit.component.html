Things to Know Before Setting Up Your MGR Plan

A Merry-Go-Round (MGR) plan works just like any typical contribution group such as Ajo, Adashi, or Esusu, the only difference is you get to do it digitally with more security and transparency. Before setting your plan up, ensure you have the following details - it is a good idea to speak to the people you want to join your group ahead of time so you all agree to the terms of the plan:

  1. The contribution duration - how frequently do you want to contribute and payout?
  2. The number of members - decide on the number of people you want to join.
  3. Amount - this is the sum each person will donate at the interval you decide (weekly, monthly, etc.)
  4. Contribution Start Date - this is the date the system will start collecting contributions via the bank / card details provided. It is a good idea to select a date that gives members enough time to have their payment methods set up and their funds ready.
  5. Allocation Date - this is the date when the total sum contributed will be paid out to the member whose turn it is to receive it.
  6. Join Date Deadline - Pick a date that gives members sufficient time to join. The plan will start without any member who has not joined at this date.
  7. Allotment type - You can manually allocate collection slots to members or it can be done automatically by the system. Members can also swap slots.
